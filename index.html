How to Play

Guess the Colourdle code in 8 tries.

Click on each square to cycle through the colours to choose your code.

Hit the enter button to submit your guess.

After each guess, the two hint tiles on the right of the row will show how many colours have been guessed correctly and in the right spot (green) and how many colours have been guessed correctly but in the wrong spot (yellow).
